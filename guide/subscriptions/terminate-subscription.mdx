---
title: "Terminate a subscription"
description: "You can terminate a subscription at any time. This will cancel the active subscription."
---

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Dashboard">
    To perform an upgrade or a downgrade through the user interface:
    1. Access the **"Customers"** section via the side menu;
    2. Select a customer from the list;
    3. In the **"Overview"** tab. By clicking the ellipse icon, you can **"Terminate subscription"**; and
    9. Click **"Terminate subscription"** to cancel a subscription.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API">
    <CodeGroup>
    ```bash Terminate a subscription
        curl --request DELETE \
        --url https://api.getlago.com/api/v1/subscriptions/sub_1234567890
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

When a subscription is terminated:
- Lago automatically generates an invoice for any outstanding usage-based charges that have not been invoiced yet;
- If the subscription fee for the plan has been paid in advance, Lago automatically issues a credit note for the unused number of days.
